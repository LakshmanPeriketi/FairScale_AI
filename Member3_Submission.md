# Member 3: The Interpreter (Submission Documents)

## 🌍 UN SDG 10 Impact Narrative (Reduced Inequalities)
**How FairScale AI Solves UN Goal 10:**
Automated Artificial Intelligence models used in modern banking often inherit historical biases, disproportionately rejecting loan applications from minorities or specific zip codes. While these black-box AI models make decisions faster, they exacerbate wealth inequalities. 

FairScale AI acts as a digital ethics consultant that directly addresses **UN SDG 10 (Reduced Inequalities)**. We are ensuring automated fairness for historically marginalized groups by providing human-readable explanations for AI rejections. By running a secondary "Ethics Model" and utilizing Generative AI (Gemini) to explain *why* a primary model rejected an applicant, we give banking managers the power to instantly "Heal" biased decisions. Instead of blindly trusting a discriminatory AI, managers can approve loans based on pure merit and skills, bridging the economic gap and promoting inclusive financial growth.
